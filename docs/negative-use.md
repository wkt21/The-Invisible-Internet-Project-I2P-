Negative uses and risks
•	Misuse: criminals and marketplaces sometimes use I2P for illicit purposes (a risk common to anonymous networks).[arstechnica +1]
•	Performance: higher latency and long warm-up times; requires continuous uptime for good performance.[i2p]
•	OPSEC pitfalls: browser cross-contamination, WebRTC or other leaks if misconfigured; follow dedicated-profile rules.
